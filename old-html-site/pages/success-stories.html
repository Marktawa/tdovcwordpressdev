Success Stories

Janet Njalika

Janet is the fourth born in her family. She is being kept by both parents. She has a hearing impairment. Her father does not work. He only does piece works. She was just being kept at home without going to school, because the father could not afford to pay for school fees, but since she was enrolled by Lillian Fond, she is now in school doing grade 4. She is doing very well in sign language.

The family is thanking you for the support you have been giving to her may the almighty God bless you as you continue supporting her.

Nalishebo Mumba

Nalishebo is the first born in a family of one boy. He is a double orphan. He is being kept by the grandmother who is a widow and she does not work. The grandmother once in while goes out to do piece work. Nalishebo does not talk but can hear. He also has a mental disability. He was just being kept at home without going to school because the guardian could not manage to pay his school fees. Since he was enrolled by the Lillian Fond he is now in school doing grade 2. He is doing well in sign language. The family is really appreciating you for the support you have been giving to the family. May God the almighty bless you as you continue supporting him.

Betty Mwelwa

Betty is the second born in the family of 5. She is being kept by the uncle who is not in informal employment. She is a single orphan her mother stays away at the island. She is mentally retarded. She was being kept at home because the uncle could not afford to pay for her school fees, but since she was enrolled by the Lillian fond, she is now in school doing grade 5. She is doing very well at school. She doing well in mathematics. The family is thanking for the support you have been giving to them may the good Lord bless you as you continue supporting her thank you.

Winfridah Hamuzya

Winfrida is the fourth born in a family of seven (7) she is being kept by both parents. The father is totally blind. She was born physically fit but, when she was 7 years old, she had eye problems she was taken to hospital to seek medical attention but it was in vain. Lastly, she became totally blind. The family earns their living through the mother who does some piece work. She was just being kept at home without going to school because of financial problem. Since she was enrolled by Lilian Fonds, she is now in school doing grade 4. She is doing very well at school through braille. She can write and read.

May the almighty God Bless You as you continue supporting her.

Joseph Mayani

Joseph is the fourth born in a family of six. He is physically disabled. He moves on a wheelchair. He also has short sight. He is an orphan who is being kept by the mother, who does not work. The family earns their living through the mother who is doing a small business being kept at home without going to school, but now he is in school after being enrolled by the Lilian Fonds doing grade 8. He is doing very well at school. The family is appreciating you for the support you have been rendering to them. God Bless you as you continue giving support.

Victor Mangaba

Victor is the fourth born in a family of seven. He is being kept by both parents. The father in not in formal employment. The family earns their living through the father who sells charcoal.

Victor has low vision. He was just being kept at home without going to school because; the family could not afford to pay for his school fees. He was enrolled by Lilian Fonds in 2014 and now he is is school doing grade 8. His Performance at school is very good especially in English. The family is really appreciating the Lilian Fonds for the support they have been giving to the family. God bless you as you continue supporting the family.

Thank you.
