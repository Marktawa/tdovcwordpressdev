Accessibility

Accessibility Policy

Summary

We assert that websites including web app technologies should be all inclusive. Every person should manage to access the website regardless of their disability. This of course includes our own people.

Our Policy

Our objective with regards to accessibility is to ensure everyone can access our website regardless of technology or ability. We prescribe to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1) to make sure the website is accessible and usable. This means we prioritize making our website easy for everyone to use, including persons with impairments to their:

  • Vision
  • Hearing
  • Mobility
  • Thinking and understanding

Current Status

We've worked hard on this website and assert we've achieved the goal of Level A accessibility. We monitor and evaluate our website periodically to maintain this. However, if you find any problems, please inform us.

Limitations and Alternatives

Although we strive to makes sure our website is fully accessible, there may be some limitations. Here's a summary of known limitations.

HTML Validation

Some of the site's pages do not use HTML which is valid. We have a developer in house to address these issues. However, these pages of the website should not prevent anyone from accessing the content at present.

PDFs

Users may find PDFs which are not correctly tagged. Therefore there may be some difficulty in viewing these pages for persons using assistive technologies. Our team is working hard to augment the ease of accessibility for PDFs.

Conformance Date

This accessibility statement was updated on 1 July 2021.
